Every Friday - 8pm to 11pm

Come and see the local Riamosa Fireflies, native to Myanmar. This firefly have one of the most intriguing mating pattern as they fly in the shape of a heart to attract their mate. Free viewing only for the residents of the Golden Lake Hotel
